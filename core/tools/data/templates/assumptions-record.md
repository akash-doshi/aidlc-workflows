## Open assumptions

Claims about the future that only building, running, or showing something
can settle — riskiest first, re-ranked as evidence lands. Each entry carries
an id (AS-n), the claim, its type (desirability, usability, feasibility,
viability), its origin (concept-implied, moved from the
open-questions-record, team-stated, or model-suggested — permanently marked
as such), what it rests on (an answered question, a current-state
observation, a design-language-record entry, or a mandate the
intent-statement records), the condition that
would disprove it (mandatory before an entry leaves proposed), the test that
tests it, and its status.

| Id | Claim | Type | Origin | Rests on | Disproved if | Test | Status |
|----|-------|------|--------|----------|--------------|------|--------|

## Settled

Supported or refuted entries, with the evidence that settled them — sample
and setting stated — and the disposition of their test artifacts (keep,
promote, discard).
